[Hook]
I feel the fire when your eyes meet mine
A late-night whisper sending shivers down my spine
We’re shadows dancing in this faded light
I want to hold you till the morning takes the night

[Verse 1]
The room is quiet but my heart beats loud
Just you and me away from every crowd
You touch my hand and start a slow-burn spark
A quiet comfort in the middle of the dark

[Pre-Chorus]

I’m falling deeper into what we found

[Chorus]
